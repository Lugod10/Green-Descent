Our Game features

Game developer

At Green Descent, we have chosen GDevelop as our development platform for one simple reason: it is the ideal tool to carry out our vision of an environmental survival video game.The platform allows ideas to be prototyped in a matter of minutes, which has been crucial to designing the variety of ecosystems and unique mechanics that characterize our game. Every decision and every interaction has been carefully polished to offer the best possible experience.

Gameplay: Survival and Decision Making in Real Time

In Green Descent, the gameplay focuses on survival in unpredictable environments, where every action counts. You'll explore from lush jungles to arid deserts, where extreme events like wildfires or flash floods put your life in danger. The most innovative is the dynamic decision system, where each choice directly affects the ecological balance. Gathering resources, building shelters, and interacting with transgenic creatures will challenge your skills and ethics.

Immersive Graphics and Visual Style

Green Descent stands out with its stunning high-quality 2D graphics that combine a realistic art style with immersive atmospheric details. Each ecosystem, from lush forests to desolate transgenic areas, is carefully designed to immerse the player in a world in decline. The game has a visual palette that evolves according to the state of the environment, showing the transition from a healthy environment to one in crisis. Visual effects, such as violent storms or wildfires, heighten the tension and sense of urgency in every moment of the experience.

Software

In summary, the development of Green Descent has been possible thanks to the flexibility and power of GDevelop. From creating a responsive, dynamic world to integrating advanced physics and sound systems, the software has enabled our vision for the game to become a reality. By using GDevelop, we have been able to focus on the narrative and the player experience, without worrying about technical limitations, resulting in an immersive video game that not only entertains, but also educates and raises awareness about the environmental challenges facing our planet. .
